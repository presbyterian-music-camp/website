Board Meeting Summary - June 12 2021

We have a theme for virtual PMC!

Music Camp Trinity: Faith, Hope, and Love

The dates are August 22 – 29.

We are developing the schedule in the next few weeks. There will be some repeat performances of the things you love best, some updated versions of your favourite activities, and we have some great ideas for completely new programs! More info will be available soon!

If you want to participate in leadership or behind the scenes, please let us know! Send a message to m.alison.cooper@gmail.com to discuss what you can help with.

Memorial for Marc Cooper

Camp Tamarack is planning the Marc Cooper Virtual Tribute Event on June 22 from 7:30 – 8:30 PM. Go to Camp Tamarack’s Facebook page to get the link for this event. 

The PMC Board has contributed to this memorial event. We are also in the early stages of planning something for our virtual camp. We’ll have more information soon.
