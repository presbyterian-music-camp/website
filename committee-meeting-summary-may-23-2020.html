Committee Meeting Summary - May 23 2020

You have probably heard the sad news already… there will be no in-person music camp in 2020. This hard decision was taken out of our hands by the Government of Ontario. They understand the importance of summer camps, but this year it just wasn’t safe to go ahead.

We have emailed all currently registered campers, and campers from 2017, 2018, and 2019, and also have a video message available:

https://www.presbyterianmusiccamp.ca/this-year.html

We understand that this will be very hard for our camp community. PMC is seen by many as the end of one year and the start of the next. You may be feeling angry), incredulous), or even a bit depressed. The PMC Committee shares these feelings (and many more), and we are still working on accepting that “camp” will be a different sort of experience this year.

However…there is a silver lining:

  • Our friends at Camp Tamarack (Rick, Ellen, and Marc) are optimistic that they will
    survive and thrive, and they are looking forward to hosting us again in 2021.
  • The PMC Committee will continue to work on the administrative issues (constitution,
    charitable status, emergency procedures, and more) so we will be in great shape for
    camp in 2021.
  • We have started thinking about some virtual activities for 2020 to keep our community
    engaged and active. You’ll hear more about these as the plans develop.

So, hold that time at the end of August, and watch for more news from us,

Your PMC Committee
