Committee Meeting Summary - January 2020

Neither snow, nor rain, nor heat, nor gloom of night shall keep your PMC committee from staying on schedule.

Most importantly, we confirmed that the camp dates will NOT be changed – PMC 2020 will be August 23-30 as planned.

The theme for 2020 is

The long and dusty road: exploring life as a pilgrimage

If you immediately thought of the road at Tamarack, you don’t need an illustration. Sub-themes will include:

  • Pilgrimage in community
  • Pilgrimage that changes directions
  • Pilgrimage through the wilderness
  • Pilgrimage of baby steps
  • Pilgrimage of discipleship
  • Pilgrimage that we are never walking alone

Stay tuned as we develop these themes.

The new website is very close to ready – in fact, by the time you get this the website will likely be operational. You will be redirected automatically from musicamp.ca to the new site, presbyterianmusiccamp.ca. Check it out (and let us know if you find any issues – it’s a work in progress).

The new website won’t have 2020 fees until we finalize our HST plans and the Tamarack rates for 2020. We will have fees for 2020 available by the end of February (after our next meeting).

We spent time January 25 developing a draft schedule for PMC 2020. Your feedback on last year’s schedule was very helpful, and we are excited about some new workshops for 2020 – we just need to confirm leadership for all of these plans.
