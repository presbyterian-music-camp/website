Fund Info

Alison Stewart-Patterson Fund

This fund was set up many years ago to honour Alison Stewart-Patterson, the founder of music camp. Revenue for the fund comes from earmarked donations, the Sunday worship offering (unless otherwise specified by the donor), and tuck profits.

Moncrieff Memorial Fund

The Moncrieff Memorial fund originated several years ago to honour Wilf Moncrieff, a former music camper and dean. Revenue for the fund comes from earmarked donations.

Tom Gemmell Fund

Established in grateful memory of The Rev. Dr. Thomas Gemmell, who passed away on May 13, 2012. Tom was a devoted Presbyterian, having served congregations in Whitehorse, Oshawa, St-Laurent, Ajax, and Ottawa. Tom had a deep love of music, and Tom’s family and the Presbyterian Music Camp Committee are convinced that the Tom Gemmell Fund would be an appropriate way to remember Tom and to encourage more people to experience the joy and Christian community life of Presbyterian Music Camp.

General Fund

If you are looking to make a donation, you can direct your donation to any particular fund. If you wish to give the most flexibility to the committee in how to use your donation, please consider donating to the general fund.
